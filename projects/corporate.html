Corporate/Financial

Corporate offices and financial instututions can demand exceptional levels of inerior finish. Complex wall paneling and eainscoting, coffered ceilings, custom mouldings, specialty desks, fixtures and counters, and exotic woods help define the corporate identity. Durable and functional as well as elegant, we fabricate pieces that meet the specifications and standards of the architects and designers while serving the aesthetic and functional needs of those who spend so much of their time at the office. Our expert staff will assist you in turning your woodwork desire into reality. Your needs...your wants...our solutions.
