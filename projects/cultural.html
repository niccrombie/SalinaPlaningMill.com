Worship & Cultural/Sports

Worship and Cultural Centers often have very special architectural woodwork needs. Products can range from pews, pulpits, baptismals, altars, kneeling rails, communion rails, wall and ceiling paneling, mouldings, and even casework and counter tops in classroms. Salina Planing Mill is also a supplier to sporting venues ranging from stadium suites to lockers rooms and athletic offices. All can be provided in virtually any wood species or laminate finish you may choose.
