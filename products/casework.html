Specialists in Casework

Salina Planing Mill manufacturers WOODCASE® a proprietary line of custom institutional casework. WOODCASE® is a frameless "European" style cabinet with doweled joinery, concealed hinges, wood dovetail draw boxes and ball-bearing drawer glides. Interior and exterior finishes are typically plastic laminate or wood. Flush, stile and rail, solid wood or rim-raised panel doors and drawer fronts are available. As the leader in the region in manufacturing frameless casework, we well understand the rigors of commercial use of fine cabinetry; therefore, we purposefully select the materials and implement fabrication methods to best withstand them. Through our investment in production equipment and experience, high production volumes are attainable without sacrificing quality. Of course, our casework is custom fabricated to your specifications and design in you selection of laminate or wood.

Salina Planing Mill can also provide a wide range of counter tops in wood, plastic laminate, solid polymer, epoxy resin, stone, or stainless steel.
