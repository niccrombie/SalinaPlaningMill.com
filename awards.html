HONORABLE AWARDS

Many of our key personnel serve or have served at the chapter level as well as the national level for AWI on committees involved in Standards Development, Estimating, Training and Education, and offices including chapter president as well as National President of AWI. Salina Planing Mill is an AWI certified Premium Grade woodwork firm.
